Logos

For my logo, I wanted something fun and something with movement; nothing sharp or heavy. The handwritten script feels imperfect, like something scrawled in a sketchbook margin! The diamond comes from my name: Syamantaka which is a jewel that belongs to the Hindu God Krishna, so it felt like a natural mark to carry. The warm reds, ambers, and corals keep the palette inviting. I wanted something with lower contrast between background and mark just to softens the whole thing. Not futuristic. Not corporate.
